Squarecraft

Make your own repeating pixel art wallpaper

This was one of the first apps I put on the App Store that has recently been removed (due to forgetting to pay my Apple account bill). The idea was inspired by an old Mac OS utility that allowed users to draw their own repeating background tiles. I hope to refresh this app soon, in the meantime here it is open sourced on GitHub if anyone wants to run with it.
